Firm Resume
net.atomic was started by a group of Columbia University students as an Internet-only development group to service on-campus and community organizations.
Bright, creative and web savvy, our young team has exploited the boom in Internet related services to grow from being an academic experiment to becoming an integrated solutions provider. Working for a wide range of clients, we've built sites ranging from basic commercial web presences to sophisticated Intranet-based information systems.
We focus on our strengths.

Uncluttered by bureaucracy, our creative team has room to focus on your solutions.
We're integrated. It might seem easy just to hire some college kid to make your website.
